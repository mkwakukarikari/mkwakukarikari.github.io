Projects

Research and professional projects in transportation planning and GIS.

Atlanta Transportation Demand Management (TDM) Study

2025 – Present

Community engagement and active mobility planning for multimodal transportation options in the Greater Atlanta area. Work involves one-on-one route planning and practice trips using different transportation modes, tabling at community events, leading education sessions, and MyGCO training and demonstrations as part of the Toole Design Group internship.

Active Mobility Community Engagement Multimodal Transportation Atlanta, GA

Accra Traffic Management and Motorcycle Taxi Study

2024

An assessment of motorcycle taxis (okada) as a transport option in the urban life of Accra, Ghana. The study examines rider demographics, trip characteristics, spatial coverage, safety concerns, and regulatory frameworks. Findings inform policy recommendations for integrating informal transport into Accra's formal mobility ecosystem.

Urban Mobility Informal Transport GIS Analysis Accra, Ghana

Kumasi Bus Rapid Transit (BRT) Feasibility Study

2020

Assisted in the feasibility study for implementing a Bus Rapid Transit system in Kumasi, Ghana's second-largest city. Responsibilities included collecting and analyzing traffic data for selected BRT corridors, generating project transport maps, and contributing to route alignment and demand modeling. Conducted under ROM Transportation Engineering Limited.

Bus Rapid Transit Traffic Analysis Transport Planning Kumasi, Ghana
