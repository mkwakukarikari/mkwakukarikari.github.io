GIS & Spatial Analysis

Geospatial analyses, maps, and spatial data visualizations across transportation, urban planning, and health geography.

Category 1

Transportation & Mobility

Network & Accessibility Analysis

Applied network-based spatial analysis to model movement, accessibility, and connectivity across urban and regional systems. Techniques include shortest path routing, origin–destination (OD) matrix development and flow mapping, accessibility analysis, and network connectivity measures (e.g., Shimbel index) to support transportation and spatial planning decisions.

ArcGIS Pro QGIS R Microsoft Excel

Bus Rapid Transit (BRT) Network Planning: Accra and Kumasi

Mapped and analyzed proposed BRT corridors in Accra and Kumasi to assess connectivity, corridor coverage, and alignment with major activity centers. The map highlights priority routes and their potential to improve urban mobility and reduce congestion.

QGIS ArcGIS Pro Google My Maps

Traffic Analysis Zone (TAZ) Income Assessment for BRT Planning

Analyzed income distribution across Traffic Analysis Zones (TAZs) to evaluate equity and accessibility implications of proposed BRT corridors. The map identifies underserved low-income areas and informs route prioritization for more inclusive transit planning.

SPSS Google My Maps

Municipality of Victoria Action Planning Framework and Spatial Analysis

Developed a spatially informed action planning framework for the Municipality of Victoria by integrating GIS-based analysis of land use, infrastructure, and community needs. Produced maps to support decision-making, priority setting, and sustainable urban development strategies.
