Geospatial Analysis & Mapping

A collection of GIS analyses, spatial models, and maps produced through research and professional work.

Health Geography & Spatial Epidemiology

Spatial Clusters of Diabetes Prevalence in Alabama's Black Belt Region

2025

Spatial cluster analysis using Local Moran's I and Getis-Ord Gi* statistics to identify statistically significant hot spots and cold spots of diabetes prevalence across Alabama's Black Belt counties. Analysis integrates socioeconomic, demographic, and healthcare accessibility data to surface drivers of spatial health disparities.

ArcGIS Pro Spatial Autocorrelation Hot Spot Analysis Health Disparities Alabama

Healthcare Accessibility Mapping in Underserved Rural Areas

2025

Network-based accessibility analysis measuring travel time and distance from residential locations to primary care facilities, hospitals, and specialty clinics across Alabama's Black Belt. Identifies service gaps and underserved populations using GIS-based catchment area modeling and drive-time polygon analysis.

Network Analysis Service Area Analysis Healthcare Access ArcGIS Pro Rural Alabama

Geospatial and Socioeconomic Patterns of Diabetes Across the United States

2025

National-scale spatial analysis mapping county-level diabetes prevalence alongside socioeconomic indicators including poverty rates, health insurance coverage, food access, and physical inactivity. Uses choropleth mapping, bivariate mapping, and regression-based spatial modeling to examine geographic correlates of diabetes burden.

Choropleth Mapping Bivariate Mapping Spatial Regression National Scale R Programming

Urban Environment & Land Use

Ecosystem Service Loss and Green Space Depletion in Atlanta, Georgia

2025

Multi-temporal land use/land cover (LULC) change detection using satellite imagery to quantify the loss of green infrastructure and ecosystem services in the Atlanta metropolitan area. Analyzes urban expansion patterns, tree canopy loss, and implications for urban heat island effects and stormwater management.

LULC Change Detection Remote Sensing NDVI Analysis Urban Ecology Atlanta, GA

Wetland Destruction Trends in Three African Cities

2025

Comparative spatial analysis of wetland loss in three major sub-Saharan African cities using multi-decade satellite imagery and GIS-based change analysis. Examines the pace and spatial patterns of urban encroachment on wetland areas and the resulting sustainability and flood risk implications.

Wetland Mapping Satellite Imagery Change Detection West Africa QGIS

Densu River Basin Land Use Change Analysis, Ghana

2024

GIS-based analysis of land use and land cover change dynamics in the Densu River Basin in Ghana from 1990 to 2020. Maps the spatial extent of agricultural expansion, urban growth, and deforestation and links these changes to river basin degradation, water quality decline, and biodiversity loss.

River Basin Analysis LULC Change ArcGIS Ghana Environmental GIS

Transportation & Mobility Mapping

Motorcycle Taxi Spatial Coverage and Route Analysis, Accra

2024

Spatial mapping of motorcycle taxi (okada) operational zones, origin-destination flows, and service coverage gaps in Accra, Ghana. GIS analysis identifies underserved corridors and areas of high informal transport concentration, informing recommendations for regulatory integration into the formal transport network.

Origin-Destination Analysis Transport Mapping ArcGIS Pro Accra, Ghana Informal Mobility

BRT Corridor Mapping and Traffic Flow Analysis, Kumasi

2020

Mapping and spatial analysis supporting the Bus Rapid Transit feasibility study for Kumasi, Ghana. Generated route alignment maps, traffic volume overlays, and corridor suitability analyses for proposed BRT corridors. Contributed 50% of the project's transport maps using ArcGIS and field-collected traffic count data.

BRT Planning Traffic Volume Mapping Corridor Analysis ArcGIS Kumasi, Ghana
